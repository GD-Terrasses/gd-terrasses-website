    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of materials do you use for terraces?

    We use a range of high-quality materials, including natural wood, composite, and eco-friendly materials. Each material is chosen for its durability, aesthetics, and ability to withstand the elements, ensuring a long life and beautiful appearance for your deck.

    How long does it take to build a custom deck?

    The duration of building a custom deck depends on the size and complexity of the project. Typically, a project can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks. We will provide you with an accurate time estimate during the consultation phase.

    Can I customize the design of my terrace?

    Absolutely! We offer a custom craft service to create a deck that perfectly reflects your style and specific needs. Our experts will work with you to design a unique and personalized deck.

    Can you incorporate special features like lighting or pergolas into the deck design?

    Yes, we offer options to customize your deck with special features such as integrated lighting, pergolas, stylish railings, and more. We work with you to create a deck that perfectly meets your aesthetic and functional needs.

    How do I maintain my deck once it is installed?

    We recommend regular maintenance to ensure the longevity and beauty of your deck. This includes periodic cleaning to remove dirt and debris, as well as applying sealants or protective treatments depending on the material of your deck. We can also provide you with specific maintenance tips tailored to your deck type.

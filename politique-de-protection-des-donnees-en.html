4. Sharing and Disclosure of Data
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data may be shared with:

5. Data Storage
We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice or as required by law. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.


6. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. Despite these measures, please note that no security measure is completely foolproof..


7. Your Rights
In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:

Access: You can request access to your personal data and obtain a copy of it.
Rectification: You can request the correction of your inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure: You may request erasure of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
Restriction: You may request restriction of the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Opposition: You can object to the processing of your data under certain conditions.
Portability: You can request the transfer of your data to another organization, where technically feasible.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].
